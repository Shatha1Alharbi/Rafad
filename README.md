# Rafad – Smart Healthcare Management System

## Overview
Rafad is a smart healthcare management system designed to support patients with chronic conditions through medication adherence, health monitoring, caregiver coordination, and accessibility-focused healthcare services.

The system helps users manage medications, track health readings, receive reminders and alerts, generate reports, and communicate with caregivers through a user-centered and accessible digital experience.

---

## Project Objectives
- Improve medication adherence for patients
- Support chronic disease monitoring and health tracking
- Enhance healthcare accessibility and usability
- Facilitate caregiver-patient coordination
- Provide intelligent and user-friendly healthcare services

---

## Key Features
- Medication reminders and tracking
- Blood pressure and blood sugar recording
- Health and statistical report generation
- Caregiver access management
- Accessibility settings based on WCAG standards
- AI-powered healthcare chatbot
- Alert and notification system
- Secure authentication and email validation
- User profile and account management
